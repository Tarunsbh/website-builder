ENJOY THE Comfort & Hospitality

Umraogarh is located on the outskirts of the city in the high end is a unique stone’s work with a lush green gardens and 24/7 room facility with unmatched services. It is ideally located on Jaipur-Jaisalmer Bypass at Chopasni in Jodhpur. Umraogarh is an multi-purpose resort. ‘Garh’ signifies a small fortress or feudal territory – a complex with residence. Umraogarh is an artistically developed properly for hassle free holiday, Family functions, Group outings and Weddings and many more. It resorts princely ambience and presents an eco friendly haunts for Bird-Watchers. Here at Umraogarh there are many birds like Red Wattled Lapwing, Rock Pigeons, Collared Doe, Indian Rollers, Kingfishers and many more. This resort is essentially an abode for a luxurious experience for traditional hospitality “Marwar” is widely known for.
